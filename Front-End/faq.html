Q1)Are you an NGO?
ANS)No, we are not an NGO. We are just an aggregator who interacts with the social organizations like NGOs so that the donors will have the privilege of a hassle-free donation process.

Q2)What benefits do your users get?
ANS)When a user donates something, we keep track of his number of donations and after some particular streak of donation, he/she is rewarded with some discount coupon or voucher.

Q3)Is this website of yours free to use?
ANS)Yes, this website of course is free to use. Its not made just for the users who can donate, but also for the awareness of the people regarding reusing of not-so-needy things.
Q4)Do you have any android or iOS compatible application for your beloved users?
ANS)Yes, we do have an android as well as iOS based application for our dear users who are so willing to support for this noble cause.

Q5)Why should we use this specific website ar app for donating rather than using the NGO-specific websites?
ANS)Most of the NGO-specific websites/apps in India are not so user-friendly and the process for donation takes a lot of time, so inspired by the 'Make In India' projects, this project is specially made for the fellow noble Indians who should be appreciated by saving their valuable time and rewarding them for their good deeds.

Q6)On what basis do you give rewards(coupons or vouchers) to your users?
ANS)We keep atrack of the users' donation streak and check with their leaderboards to give away the rewards.
